About Me

Postdoctoral Researcher specializing in AI-driven mobility and communication systems, spanning 5G/6G, V2X, distributed AI, simulation platforms, and intelligent transportation. Currently affiliated with the CORE-AI Lab (Physical Embodied AI), with a core focus on on-device AI and edge intelligence, developing Agentic AI systems for adaptive, resource-aware, and autonomous optimization in real-world environments.

Skilled in software development, SDR prototyping, and network simulation using ns-3, MATLAB, and Python-based platforms. Proficient in advanced AI/ML methods including Deep Reinforcement Learning, LSTM, TD3, and DDPG for system-level optimization and real-time decision making.

Actively engaged in 3GPP-aligned R&D and 5GAA collaboration, with contributions to sidelink standardization, intelligent resource allocation, and vehicular communication protocol design.

Experienced in developing C-CAV-focused software frameworks integrating sensor fusion, context-aware semantics extraction, and Agentic AI–based perception and reasoning layers, with emphasis on on-device LLM optimization for thermally-aware, energy-efficient, and scalable Physical Embodied AI systems enabling next-generation cooperative and automated mobility.

My broader goal is to build practical, efficient, and standards-aware communication frameworks for future connected and autonomous systems.
