About Me

Embodied Physical AI Researcher specializing in AI-driven mobility and communication systems, spanning 5G/6G, V2X, distributed AI, simulation platforms, and intelligent transportation. Currently affiliated with the CORE-AI Lab (Physical Embodied AI), with a core focus on on-device AI and edge intelligence, developing Agentic AI systems for adaptive, resource-aware, and autonomous optimization in real-world environments.

Skilled in software development, SDR prototyping, and network simulation using ns-3, MATLAB, and Python-based platforms. Proficient in advanced AI/ML methods including Deep Reinforcement Learning, LSTM, TD3, and DDPG for system-level optimization and real-time decision making.
